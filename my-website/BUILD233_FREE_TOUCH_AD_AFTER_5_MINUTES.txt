BUILD 233 – FREE MEMBER TOUCH POP AD

- Pop ad is enabled for FREE members only.
- VIP members receive no pop ad.
- The five-minute timer starts when playback or download is requested.
- After five minutes, the ad triggers only on the user’s next screen touch/pointer interaction.
- After triggering, another five-minute waiting period begins.
- Removed the old immediate player pop-ad injection.
