CINEFLEX BUILD 8.0.6 — 3-HOUR WATCH TIME

Changes:
- Every brand-new signed-in user starts with 3 hours (10,800 seconds).
- Every successful Add Time claim adds another 3 hours.
- Add Time was moved to the upper-right homepage navigation.
- VIP members continue to have unlimited watch time and the Add Time button is hidden.
- Existing users keep their current stored balance; only users without a watchTime/balance document receive the new 3-hour initial grant.

Firestore path:
users/{uid}/watchTime/balance
Field: remainingSeconds
