CINEFLEX BUILD 7.1 — MONETAG WATCH TIME

Monetag MultiTag zone:
259251

Integrated script:
https://quge5.com/88/tag.min.js

FLOW
1. Signed-in user receives an initial 15-minute balance.
2. Balance decreases only while the embedded player is active and the tab is visible.
3. When the balance reaches zero, the stream is held and the Add Watch Time modal appears.
4. The user taps “Support CineFlex • +15 Minutes.”
5. Monetag MultiTag may display an ad according to its own optimization and frequency rules.
6. CineFlex runs a 20-second on-page support countdown. The countdown pauses while the tab is hidden.
7. After the countdown, 15 minutes are added and the held movie resumes.

IMPORTANT
- MultiTag does not provide CineFlex with a verified rewarded-video completion callback.
- The site therefore does not claim that a video ad was completed.
- The added time is tied to completion of the visible CineFlex support countdown.
- Do not add another all-in-one ad network script to the same page without testing conflicts.
- Keep the existing Firestore watch-time rules from BUILD 7.0.
