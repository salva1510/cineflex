CINEFLEX BUILD 14.0 — PERFORMANCE & PREMIUM POLISH

Added:
- Lazy loading and async decoding for poster images
- Fade-in image loading states
- Lightweight row skeleton placeholders
- Offscreen rendering optimization using content-visibility
- Smooth card and page transitions
- Mobile momentum scrolling and scroll snapping
- Reduced-motion accessibility support
