CINEFLEX BUILD 9.5 — CLEAN BANNER + FULLSCREEN LIVE TV

- Removed CineFlex, Live viewers, Smart pick, and star metric badges from the hero banner.
- Removed the Trending, Blockbusters, Anime Rush, K-Drama Night, Horror Night, and Family Time shortcut strip below the banner.
